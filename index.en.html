Fundación Ciudadana Civio

Annual report 2016

At Civio (Fundación Ciudadana Civio: Civio Citizen Foundation) we demand accountability from public institutions, and with this report, we lead by way of example. Here, you will find detailed information about everything we have done over the past year, what worked and what didn't. This is our 3rd Annual Report (the 2014 and 2015 reports are also accessible), an exercise we undertake each year with enthusiasm, honesty and a critical eye in order to maintain the trust of our many supporters and gain that of more people every day.

The Civio team.
